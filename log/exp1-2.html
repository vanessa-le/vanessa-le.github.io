About Life in Lockdown (alt. title After Tove Jansson)

Some sketches of a book that won't be printed anytime soon.

Some questions, going into and as a result of the experiments:

☛What part do specific book design conventions play in a reading experience? ☛What happens when they are utilised for other purposes? ☛What other devices can you use to express a narrative in a book? ☛What would it look like if a book was dissected? ☛How does altering the form of book affect the reading experience?
