IM/PERMANENCE, 140 pages, 203 × 254 mm, 2020; for Monash University, Faculty of Fine Art
This publication was made as a catalogue of work and keepsake for the soon-to-be graduates of the Fine Art program at Monash University. It was conceptualised through ongoing conversations with a committee of students, in which we discussed the loss of collective studio spaces and transitioning to working online.
Postscript, 60 pages, 176 × 250 mm, 2018
This is a speculative publication that begins with an old album full of stamps. It not only archives this collection of ephemera, but also explores personal and local histories. The course of this investigation is documented through existing material, photographs and words.
Exegesis, 36 pages, 135 × 264 mm, 2020
Through writing, scanned imagery and a collection of audio-visual sources, this book is a documentation and culmination of the process of my honours research project. Throughout this year I explored and consolidated how and why I design, through the lens of design theory and other fields of knowledge, namely writing and conceptual art.
Phở, 56 pages, 176 × 250 mm, 2018
A riso printed publication that examines the various historical and cultural contexts of phở, from its unclear origins to its recent popularisation. This includes my own observations and anecdotes of the dish.
Colour Diary, 64 pages, 176 × 250 mm, 2018
Part sketchbook, retrospective journal and colour swatch compilation, this publication collects fragments of a trip to Japan and South Korea.
